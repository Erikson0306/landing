The Authors

Erik Hansen

Erik Hansen is a final year student at WHU- Otto Beisheim School of Management. Through several internships in the global executive search sector, he has developed a keen interest in the various challenges faced by executives in the global economy. He will complete his Master's degree at WHU and is interested in practical experience in the field of international strategy.

Maximilian Müller

Maximilian Müller is also a final year student at WHU- Otto Beisheim School of Management and held the position of Student Representative. In this role he learned early on about the importance of effective organizational communication. After numerous internships in the investment and PE industry, he would like to use his experience in the context of this research work to advise that same industry in the field of communication.

Supervisor

Lukas Löhlein took up his position as WHU Assistant Professor at the Institute of Management Accounting and Control (IMC) in March 2017. Lukas Löhlein received his doctorate from the University of Luxembourg (Luxembourg) in 2016, after first completing his studies in management and political science. Before coming to WHU, Lukas Löhlein worked as a post-doc at the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE) from 2015 to 2017.
